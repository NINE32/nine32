Gilmour's Garage

Scope: Graphic design

Gilmour's Garage, owned and run by Ken Gilmour, in California, USA, has been building and restoring all sorts of older vehicles for decades.

Ken asked for a graphic to be displayed on the side of his personal shop truck, in a vintage style to represent the era of vehicles he works on.
