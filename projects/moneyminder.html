MoneyMinder

Logo design, UI/UX design

Born out of a desire to take control of spending habits and recognising the shared challenges faced by friends in managing their finances, I set out to create a solution that would assist in the management of day-to-day spending.

From envisioning the logo, to the user interface and fine-tuning the functionality, every aspect of MoneyMinder has been meticulously crafted with user experience in mind. With intuitive features and a sleek design, this app is poised to become your trusted companion on the path towards financial freedom.
